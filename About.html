About Meeting Point

Meeting Point is a fast food center dedicated to serving delicious, fresh, and affordable food for everyone. We believe that good food brings people together, and our goal is to provide quick service without compromising on quality or taste.

From spicy biryanis and fried rice to tasty noodles and snacks, our menu is designed to satisfy every craving. Whether you are dining with friends, family, or grabbing a quick bite, Meeting Point is the perfect place.
